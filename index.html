The Packaging Crisis

E-commerce is booming, but the environmental cost is rising. Single-use plastic and inefficient logistics are no longer sustainable.

Critical Waste Levels

Packaging accounts for a significant portion of global waste. Conventional plastics take hundreds of years to decompose, clogging landfills and oceans.

Growing Demand

The global sustainable packaging market was valued at $273 Billion USD in 2023 and is projected to reach $449 Billion by 2030 (Grand View Research).

Consumer Shift

According to McKinsey & NielsenIQ, consumers increasingly reward brands that prioritize sustainability, making green packaging a competitive necessity.

The EcoPax Definition

Packaging-as-a-Service (PaaS)

We don't just sell boxes; we provide a closed-loop ecosystem. EcoPax offers a subscription service for e-commerce SMEs and Direct-to-Consumer brands, combining high-quality materials with reverse logistics.

Innovative Products

Certified home-compostable mailers and durable reusable boxes designed for up to 50 cycles.

Returns Management

Branded Design
