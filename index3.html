Market Validation

We conducted a consumer perception survey (N=150+) to validate the demand for sustainable packaging. The data confirms that packaging waste is a major pain point and consumers are ready for a change, though price remains a sensitivity.

Key Survey Findings

  • High Awareness: 85% of respondents consider packaging waste a major environmental issue.
  • Price Sensitivity: While willing to pay more, 65% cap their premium at 5%, suggesting our PaaS cost-sharing model is vital.
  • Trust Gap: Moderate trust in green labels indicates a need for our verified certification service (EN 13432).
