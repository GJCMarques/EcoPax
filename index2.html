Frequently Asked Questions

How does the reusable model work?

Customers receive the product in an EcoPax reusable mailer. They flip the label and drop it in any post box. We collect, sanitize, and restock it for you.

What certifications do you hold?

We pursue EN 13432 (Europe) and ASTM D6400 (US) for compostability standards to ensure no greenwashing.

Can you integrate with Shopify?

Yes. Our API connects directly with Shopify and WooCommerce to trigger packaging fulfillment automatically.
